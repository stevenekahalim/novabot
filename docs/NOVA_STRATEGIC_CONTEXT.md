## 1. APEX BUSINESS MODEL

## 6. DOMAIN KNOWLEDGE

### Padel Court Specs
- Standard dimensions: 20m x 10m per court
- Minimum height: 6-7m
- Construction cost: 300-500jt per court
- Timeline to build: 2-3 months from design approval

### Legal Requirements (Indonesia)
- **PT/CV formation**: 2-4 weeks, cost ~15-20jt
- **IMB (building permit)**: 1-2 months depending on location
- **Business licenses**: Trade license (SIUP), company registration (TDP)
- **Bank account**: Requires PT/CV completion

### Venue Types
- **Mall-based**:
  - Pros: High traffic, premium market, security
  - Cons: Higher rental, limited hours, mall regulations
  - Economics: 20-30jt/month rental, faster ROI

- **Standalone**:
  - Pros: Full control, 24/7 operations possible
  - Cons: Need to drive traffic, security costs
  - Economics: 15-20jt/month rental, slower traffic buildup
